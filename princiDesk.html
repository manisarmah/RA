Principal's Desk

Keshab Mahanta, Principal

Considering the long felt need of a Science Academy imparting quality education along with the purpose of inculcating the basic human values among the young generation Ramanujan Academy is established.

We believe Careful grooming and utmost guidance to the students by esteemed and qualified teachers will always help the students to stand at par in competitions in various fields like Olympiad , all competitive exams, Medical and Engineering entrance exams, etc. We, the family of Ramanujan Academy is committed to establish this institution as one of the renowned and trusted name in the field of quality education.
